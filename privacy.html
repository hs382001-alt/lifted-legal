Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lifted ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple or Google, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Unique user identifier

Fitness and Activity Data

  • Workout routines you create
  • Exercise logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  • Workout history and personal records
  • Check-in photos and timestamps
  • Daily prompts completed
  • Streak and level progress

Group Data

  • Groups you create or join
  • Group invite codes
  • Group member information
  • Shared workouts and check-ins
  • Group activity and interactions

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Usage analytics (optional)

Photos and Camera

  • Check-in photos taken within the app
  • Photos are stored on our servers and visible to your group members

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the app experience
  • Enable workout tracking and progress monitoring
  • Facilitate group accountability features
  • Display check-in photos to group members
  • Send notifications about group activity and reminders
  • Provide customer support
  • Analyze app usage to improve features
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Within Groups

Check-in photos, workout logs, and activity are visible to all members of your groups. This is essential to the app's accountability features.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for:

  • Authentication (Apple Sign In, Google Sign In)
  • Cloud storage and database hosting
  • Analytics (optional and anonymized)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and users' safety.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers
  • Check-in photos are stored for the duration of your group participation
  • Workout data is stored indefinitely unless you delete your account
  • We implement industry-standard security measures, but no system is 100% secure

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the app
  • Edit your profile information and display name
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Opt out of promotional notifications
  • Leave groups and stop sharing data with those members

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Check-in photos: Retained while you're in the associated group
  • Workout history: Retained until you delete your account
  • Deleted accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days

Children's Privacy

Lifted is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

Users aged 13-17 should use the app with parental guidance and consent.

International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using Lifted, you consent to such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
