How do I create a group?

Tap the Groups tab, then tap "Create Group". Give your group a name and share the invite code with your friends so they can join.

How do I join a group?

Tap the Groups tab, then tap "Join Group". Enter the invite code shared by your group creator and you'll be added immediately.

What happens if I miss a check-in?

If you miss your daily check-in, your group character resets to Level 1. You can use a Revive (available with Lifted Revive subscription) to restore your progress.

What is Lifted Revive?

Lifted Revive is our premium subscription ($4.99/month or $49.99/year) that gives you one character revive per month, plus access to custom frames, backgrounds, character skins, and reactions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your iPhone Settings:

  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Tap "Lifted Revive"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
How do I restore my purchases?

If you reinstall the app or switch devices, tap "Restore Purchases" in Settings to restore your subscription.

How do I create a workout routine?

Tap the Track tab, then tap "Create Routine". Name your routine, add exercises from our 300+ exercise database, and save. You can then start a workout directly from your saved routine.

How do I log a workout?

Go to the Track tab, select your routine, and tap "Start Workout". Log your sets, reps, and weight, check them off as you complete them, then save when done. You can also share your workout to your group feed.

How do I take a check-in photo?

Tap the Check-In button on your home screen or group feed. Follow the unique daily prompt, take your photo, and submit. Your check-in will appear in your group feed.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently delete all your data including workout history, check-ins, and group memberships. This cannot be undone.

You can also email us at liftedappios@gmail.com to request account deletion.

The app is crashing or not working properly

Try these steps:

  1. Close and reopen the app
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Update to the latest version in the App Store
  4. Restart your iPhone
  5. Delete and reinstall the app

If the problem persists, email us at liftedappios@gmail.com with a description of the issue.

I didn't receive my premium features after subscribing

Try tapping "Restore Purchases" in the app settings. If that doesn't work, email us at liftedappios@gmail.com and we'll sort it out within 24 hours.

How do I track supplements?

Go to the supplements section in the app, tap "Add Supplement", enter the name, dosage, and timing. You can log your daily intake and set reminders to never miss your stack.

Why can't I see my group members' check-ins?

Make sure you're connected to the internet. Pull down on the feed to refresh. If the problem continues, try closing and reopening the app.
